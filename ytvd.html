Youtube Video
Downloader

Project

The main Idea behin the porject was my inability to find a trusted source to download my favourite youtube videos. Since most of them were full of malicious adds and malwares. So, I thought, Why not have a downloader of my own?!

This was an interesting project based on the python youtube_dl module. I implemented the project on Django. With the front-end based on HTML and CSS.

I received the data from the youtube_dl module in the form of a JSON file same as an API, and thus I managed to fetch the useful ata to provide my users with every other video and audio format of their favourite video.
