MY Time at AIESEC

An amazing Experience.

AIESEC is the world's biggest Youth Run Non-Government organisation, having chapters in 120+ countries around the world. Working with the motive to achieve world peace by conducting cross-cultural experiences between various countries.

I was a part of the Local Chapter of AIESEC in Indore. Being a part of the Outgoing Global Volunteer(oGV) Programme, I was elated to be a partof a great team and I was able to make a lot of connections both in India and outside India. I was to interact with lovely folks from 10+ countries like Turkey, Indonesia, etc.

Though the COVID restricted my experience to a virtual one, but it was a great experience. Knoowing people from various cultures, disciplines and learning new stuff from every new guy you meet. It's an experience everyone should have once in their life.
